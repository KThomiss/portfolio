Location

Admission

The museum is located at: Museumsgate 24 3183 Horten

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These hours are 70NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a cafe attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffe, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you have left the museum
